This is the application which I developed in my free time. I developed this application because none of the free music players in the android play store were good. The music player uses fragments, intent, pending intent and widgets. It uses a foreground service for music playback, pending intent, fragments. The UI has several activities. The main activity uses a view pager to show different fragments.

Detailed Description

Axif is a music player having all features like equalizer, search, and great and simple user interface. It gives you access to essentials like albums, artists, playlist and more. The music player has two widgets which let you control song playback and equalizer. It supports mp3, aac, wav, flac and many other audio formats. Note that it is a music player not a music downloader. It cannot download any music.

Key Features

  • Browse and play your music by albums, artists, songs, playlists.
  • 2 widgets (4 X 2, 4 X 1) – one gives you access to music playback and the other gives control over equalizer and playback.
  • Easy playlist creation and browsing.
  • Notification view for controlling your music playback. Play, pause, previous, album art and more controls.
  • Equalizer with support up to 8 bands (depends on the device). Presets for quick effect.
  • Headset – playback pauses and resumes on being plugged and unplugged.
  • Fast and low memory consumption.
  • Music Shuffle and cycle modes.
